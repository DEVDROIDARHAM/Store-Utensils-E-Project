Care for Your Kitchen Tools

Simple tips to make your utensils last longer

Knife Care

Always hand wash your knives and dry immediately. Never soak them in water or put them in a dishwasher as it damages the blade edge and handle over time.

Non-Stick Pans

Never use metal utensils on non-stick surfaces. Always cook on low to medium heat and hand wash gently to preserve the non-stick coating for longer.

Stainless Steel

Remove water stains with a mix of vinegar and water. Always use a soft non-abrasive sponge to avoid scratching the surface and keep the shine intact.

Wooden Utensils

Wash wooden spoons with warm soapy water and dry immediately. Never put them in a dishwasher or soak in water as wood can crack and warp easily.
